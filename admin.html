- The Faster Mojito -

- The Slower Mojito -

2 ounce Light [Rum]

=== Syrups ===

- Simple Syrup -

One part [White Sugar]

One part [Water]

- Semi-Rich Simple Syrup -

One and a half parts [White Sugar]

One part [Water]

- Rich Simple Syrup -

Two parts [White Sugar]

One part [Water]

- Rich Demerara Syrup -

Two parts [Demerara Sugar]

One part [Water]

- Cinnamon Syrup -

One and a half parts [White Sugar]

One part [Water]

4 [Cinnamon Sticks]

*Simmer for 2 minutes for immediate use; 6-12 hours for stronger flavor

- Grenadine Syrup -

Three Cups [White Sugar]

Two Cups [100% Pomegranate Juice]

- Cups [Pomegranate Molasses]

0.5 tsp [Rose Water] *added after heat

- Orgeat -

Three Cups [White Sugar]

Two Cups [Unsweetened Almond Milk]

1 tsp [Orange Blossom Water]

0.5 tsp [Rose Water]

0.5 tsp [Almond Extract]

*Orange Blossom Water, Rose Water, and Almond Extract are to be added after heat
